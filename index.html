Welcome to KPOPBASE

KPOPBASE is your comprehensive guide to the South Korean entertainment industry. We provide an interactive map and detailed directory of the most influential K-pop agencies, from global powerhouses like HYBE, SM, JYP, and YG, to rising labels that are shaping the future of music.

Explore company headquarters, discover artist rosters, and check CEO information all in one place. Whether you are a dedicated fan wanting to know where your favorite group was formed, or an industry analyst looking for data, KPOPBASE connects you to the heart of Hallyu.

What is KPOPBASE?

KPOPBASE is a global directory service that helps users find information about K-pop entertainment agencies, including their location, artists, and key personnel.

We strive to keep our database updated with the latest information. Agency addresses and artist rosters are verified regularly.
